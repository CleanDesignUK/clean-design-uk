Cookie Policy

Last updated — 26 June 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, and font size) so you don’t have to re-enter them whenever you come back.

2. How Clean Design UK Uses Cookies

Type of CookiePurposeExpiry
Essential Keep the site secure, remember cookie banner choice, enable form submissions. Session / 1 year
Analytics Google Analytics 4 tracks anonymised traffic (pages viewed, device type, truncated IP). 2 years
Performance Cloudflare (_cfduid) speeds up delivery and protects against DDoS attacks. 30 days
Marketing Brevo email-marketing cookie records newsletter sign-ups if you opt-in. 6 months

3. Managing Cookies

On your first visit you’ll see our banner allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can revisit this choice anytime by clearing your browser cache or using the “Cookie settings” link in the footer.

To block or delete cookies at browser level, follow the instructions for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or your preferred browser.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some features (e.g. Calendly booking widget, YouTube video embeds) may set their own cookies. We have no control over these and recommend you check the privacy policies of those providers.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology, legislation or our business. Check this page periodically—any changes become effective as soon as they are posted.
