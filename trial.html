FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our services.

How much does a website with Clean Design UK cost?

Every project is custom-quoted. Starter sites begin around £850, while fully-featured e-commerce builds start at £2,500. After a quick discovery call, we’ll send a transparent, fixed-price proposal.

Do you handle hosting & ongoing maintenance?

Yes. Our managed hosting runs on UK data centres with daily backups, SSL, and performance monitoring. We also offer flexible care plans for updates, security and content tweaks.

Can you improve my existing WordPress site?

Absolutely. From speed optimisation and UX tweaks to full design refreshes, we’ll audit your current build and implement clear, high-impact fixes without losing data or rankings.

Do you provide copywriting & SEO?

We do. Our content team researches keywords, writes conversion-focused copy, and implements on-page SEO best practices so your site launches ready to rank and convert.

How long does a typical project take?

A brochure site averages 3–4 weeks, while larger builds run 6–8 weeks. We give you a detailed timeline and keep you in the loop with weekly progress updates.
